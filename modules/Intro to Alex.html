My Journey from Physical Therapy to Coding

You're probably wondering how a Doctor of Physical Therapy came to apply for Vets Who Code.

Lets find out. Start by scrolling over the map to see different aspects of my PT journey.

I grew up in Utah and got my BS of Exercise Science at Brigham Young University
I got my Doctorate of Physical Therapy at Angelo State University
I did clinicals in Dallas and started Travel Physical Therapy Here
I worked in GA with collegiate athletes
My last travel contract was an hour out of DC at Purcellville Orthopedic PT
I got married, started a business and ended my PT career
Things I liked about PT Things I disliked about PT
Ability to create individualized treatment plans Insurance dictated treatment and incentivized generalized treatment
Constant learning Most treatments are repetitious
Got to meet people from diverse backgrounds Limited opportunities for growth unless I started my own company

I noticed the lack of growth opportunities and difficulty in providing individualized, high quality care and decided to start my own business: Golf and Sports Physical Therapy (no I didn't create that website from HTML)
